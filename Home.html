About Organization

The Youth India placement cell is an initiative of Youth India Foundation and is the core piece in helping us reach our objective. The challenges faced by our current generation to thrive for excellence often makes them overlook their potentialities and abilities. The downward trend on the economic growth, and the growing strain on the coming generation to grab hold of opportunities become tough.

This is where we come into assistance and to overcome this grave issue that is presented to us, the Youth India Placement Cell allows the worthy and striving aspirants to help attain job and internship opportunities. We aim to be the solution to the pre-existing problem of redundancy and inefficiency. We put forward a social entrepreneurship platform to assist the students and the colleges. The Youth India Placement Cell acts as a mediator between students and prospective companies via respective colleges. We take into consideration the aptitude, skills and knack of our aspirants before reaching out to the companies.

Benefits For

Companies

Human Resources are an intangible part of every business and in the modern times great talents have become harder to find , so here comes the role of Youth India Placement Cell which liberates/exempts the companies from the hassles of market research , search of a potential candidate , filtering of various resumes , scheduling interviews and aid companies find their appropriate candidates. The recruitment cost is reduced for the companies to a major extent as we charge bare minimum costs only and ensure complete cooperation .

Benefits For

Colleges

The Youth India Placement Cell, by its collaborative work with the colleges, becomes a driving link between students and prospective companies. The systematic process of recruitment provides a reliable and an end to end assistance from the Youth India Placement Cell to the colleges. This reduces the strain on both, the colleges as well as the students. Searching costs and other expenditures are also reduced. The charges, however, remain minimal for the colleges, making the whole scheme profitable for everyone.

Benefits For

Students

The students become the main beneficiaries of this initiative as we provide them a platform where we bring the various prestigious companies of different sectors together .The aspirants will get enormous opportunities for internships and job roles as we provide them with a bundle of finest companies to choose from. Moreover they get a fair chance to explore and build their skill set with bare minimum charges.
